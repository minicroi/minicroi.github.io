文章中的常见英文（短语，缩写..）

2025-06-03
#英语 #零散
学习积累

阅读英语文章时常见以下缩写：

  1. e.g. 例如（for example）
    • Many animals, e.g., lions and tigers, are carnivores.
  2. i.e. 也就是说/即（that is to say）
    • He is a polyglot, i.e., someone who speaks several languages.
  3. viz. 即/具体来说（namely）
    • Three cities, viz. London, Paris, and Rome, are on the list.
  4. cf. 参见/比较（compare）
    • Cf. Smith (2002) for a different approach.
  5. et al. 等等/以及其他人（and others）
    • The paper was written by Johnson et al.
  6. etc. 等等（and so on）
    • We need paper, pens, etc.
  7. s.t. 通常是subject to的缩写,指：满足..的条件，在..情况下
    • minimize f(x) s.t. g(x)≤0

阅读英语文章时常见以下短语：

  1. in line with 与…一致/符合
    • This decision is in line with company policy.
    • Her results were in line with our expectations.
    • His actions are not in line with his values.
  2. in the sequel 在后续中/在续集中
    • In the sequel,the hero comes back stronger.
    • I hope they explain her past in the sequel.
    • In the sequel, we often use graph-theoretical notations for MCs which refer to the underlying digraph of M.
      1. symbol:单个的标记，用来代表某个特定的概念、对象或操作。它通常是一个字符或图形。
      2. notation:一整套用于表达思想、概念或公式的规则和方式，往往由多个symbols组成。是表达的体系。
        简单地来讲：symbol是单个字符，notation是使用字符的一套语言系统
  3. in this sense 从这个角度看/在这个意义下/就此而言
    • The system is stable. In this sense, it returns to equilibrium after small disturbances.这个系统是稳定的。就这个角度来说，它在受到小扰动后会恢复到平衡状态
  4. it follows that 由此可得/因此/可以得出
    • All humans are mortal. Socrates is a human. It follows that Socrates is mortal.所有人都会死亡。苏格拉底是人。因此可推出苏格拉底会死亡
  5. consequence of 结果/推论（学术场景）
    • As a direct consequence of Theorem 10.25 作为定理10.25的直接结果
  6. amount to 等同于/归结为
    • In practice, solving this equation amounts to finding the roots of a polynomial.实际上，解这个方程就是在找一个多项式的根
